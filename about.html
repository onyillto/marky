MISSION

Our mission is to have a Nigeria where many Nigerians are lifted out of poverty

VISION

1. To lift a greater percentage of Nigerians out of poverty

2. To help empower poor Nigerians to be self-sufficient

About Marky Elechi Foundation

Marky Elechi Foundation is a non-governmental and not-for-profit organization that works towards eradicating poverty, providing humanitarian intervention, and raising awareness on economic and environmental issues affecting the people of Nigeria. MEF's work is carried out through programming, advocacy, and youth empowerment by creating synergy with relevant individuals, organizations, and agencies.
